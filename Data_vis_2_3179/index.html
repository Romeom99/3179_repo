Deforestation of Brazil

Brazil, home to the largest rainforest in the world, and has the largest amount of area covered in Primary rainforest. These rainforests however have been under threat in recent history, through natural and unatural circumstances. As is stands Brazil is slowly losing its beautiful flora, that has become a symbol of the country.

Brazil compared to the world, on average is the largest contributer to deforestation of natural rainforests. Even when comparing to the relative area of rainforests that each country has Brazil still comes out on top.
